What is Safecoin?

Safecoin a new and exciting Community driver Komodo fork, aims at being the new standard for cryptocurrency Safety, Security, and Anonymity.

Protected by ZCASH zero-knowledge proofs, users can make 100% untraceable transactions - secured by Bitcoin‘s petahash Proof of Work - due to Safecoin’s new delayed Proof of Work (dPoW) consensus mechanism which has been re-tooled from Komodo's implmentation.
